## About the Role

Capital One is seeking a Business Analyst to join our Strategic Finance & Analytics team. You will partner with senior finance and product leaders to drive data-informed decisions across pricing, forecasting, operational efficiency, and risk. This role sits at the intersection of finance, data science, and business strategy — translating complex datasets into actionable insights that influence multi-million-dollar decisions.

## What You'll Do

- Build and maintain financial models for revenue forecasting, variance analysis, and scenario planning
- Design executive-ready KPI dashboards in Power BI and Tableau for senior leadership reviews
- Translate large datasets using SQL and Python into actionable insights for cross-functional stakeholders
- Partner with product, marketing, and operations teams to identify pricing and operational efficiency opportunities
- Lead automated reporting workflows using Power Automate, Power Query, and Python scripts
- Conduct A/B testing and statistical analysis to validate business hypotheses
- Build predictive models (regression, time-series, classification) for demand forecasting and risk classification
- Communicate findings through data storytelling and executive-ready presentations
- Standardize reporting workflows and improve data quality across teams

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Business Analytics, Statistics, or related quantitative field
- 1+ years of analytics experience (internships count)
- Proficient in **SQL** — CTEs, Window Functions, complex Joins
- Proficient in **Python** (Pandas, NumPy, Scikit-learn) and/or R
- Experience with **Power BI** or **Tableau** for dashboard development
- Strong financial modeling skills in **Excel** (advanced formulas, PivotTables, Power Query)
- Familiarity with forecasting techniques (regression, time-series)
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Authorization to work in the U.S.

## Preferred Qualifications

- Master's degree in Business Analytics, Data Science, or related field
- Hands-on experience with machine learning (Random Forest, XGBoost, Prophet)
- Experience with **Power Automate**, **Power Query**, or **VBA** for workflow automation
- Knowledge of cloud platforms (AWS, Snowflake) and data warehousing concepts
- A/B testing and causal inference experience
- Experience with **FP&A workflows** — variance analysis, budget vs actuals, financial reconciliation
- Familiarity with model explainability tools (SHAP, LIME)
- Industry experience in financial services, fintech, or e-commerce
- Project portfolio demonstrating end-to-end analytics work (GitHub, Streamlit, etc.)
- Git / version control proficiency

## Tech Stack

SQL · Python · R · Power BI · Tableau · Excel · Power Automate · Snowflake · Git · AWS · Streamlit
